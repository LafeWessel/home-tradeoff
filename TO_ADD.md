# Data Categories To Add

Items queued for future implementation. Do not start on these until explicitly requested.

## Lifestyle & Outdoors

- **Public Lands** — percentage of county/state land that is federally/state-managed public land (BLM, National Forest, National Park, State Park, etc.); acreage accessible to the public
- **Mountainousness** — terrain ruggedness / elevation profile; percentage of land above given elevation thresholds; presence of significant mountain ranges
- **Hunting availability** — game species available by state/county; season lengths; license cost; public land hunting access; overall hunting friendliness rating
- **Foraging availability** — climate and biome suitability for wild edibles; presence of foraging-friendly public lands
- **Fishing availability** — freshwater vs. saltwater access; notable fisheries; license cost; stocking programs; overall fishing friendliness rating

## Regulations & Policy

- **Firearm regulations** — permissiveness rating (permitless carry, shall-issue, may-issue, restrictive); notable restrictions (magazine limits, AWB, waiting periods); constitutional carry status

## Health

- **Cancer rates** — age-adjusted cancer incidence and mortality rates at state/county level (NCI SEER or CDC PLACES)
- **Obesity rates** — adult obesity prevalence at state/county level (CDC BRFSS or CDC PLACES)

## Environment & Agriculture

- **Plant Hardiness Zones** — USDA hardiness zone(s) for the location; relevant for gardening, homesteading, and crop growing potential
